Lemon Butter Scallops



Scallops sauteed in butter and garlic that will meth in your mouth.

Delicious scallops in a light butter and garlic sauce to melt your troubles away. Enjoy a nice and simple meal that will take only 20 mins to make.

  • Butter
  • Fresh Garlic
  • Fresh Lemon Juice
  • Scallops
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Melt butter in large skillet of medium-high heat.
  2. Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add scallops, cook each side for 3 minutes until firm and opaque.
  4. Remove scallops to make sauce with remaining butter.
  5. Whisk in salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
