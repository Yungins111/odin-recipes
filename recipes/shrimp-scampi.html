Shrimp Scampi

Light and savory, an easy shrimp recipe for a night in.

This easy shrimp scampi recipe is a simple but elegant shrimp dish. Combine it with garlic bread and a salad for a delicious dinner.

  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • Butter
  • Shrimp
  • Minced Garlic
  • Dry White Wine
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil large pot of light salted water.
  2. Cook angel hair pasta, while stirring occasionally until al dente.
  3. Melt butter in saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Cook garlic slightly until browned.
  5. Stir in shrimp, cook until pink (about 3-5 minutes).
  6. Stir in wine and pepper, bring sauce to a boil for 30 secs.
  7. Add shrimp to pasta, sprinkle parmesan cheese and toss well.
