Honey Soy Sauce Pork Chops

Sweet Succulent Juicy Pork Chops

Grilled pork chops brushed with a honey and soy sauce mix. To round out the meal, serve with creamy chive and garlic mashed potatoes.

  • Honey
  • Soy Sauce
  • Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
  • Fresh Ground Black Pepper
  • Pork Chops
  • Canola Oil
  • Salt
  • Sliced Sweet Onion

Steps

  1. Preheat grill on high heat. Lightly oil the grate.
  2. Mix honey, soy sauce, red pepper flakes, and black pepper in a bowl.
  3. Brush chops with canola oil and sprinkle salt and black pepper.
  4. Grill pork chops on hottest part of grill for 4 to 5 minutes each side
  5. Move pork chops to cooler area and brush glaze on both sides generously. Keep cooking for 3 to 4 minutes more.
